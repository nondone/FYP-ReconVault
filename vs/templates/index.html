What ReconVault Does

ReconVault helps you understand what a website exposes on the internet. It discovers related web addresses, checks which ones are online, collects public clues from the web, and flags obvious security warnings. You can review results per target and keep a history of scans.

Discover
Finds subdomains like api.example.com and checks which web services are alive.
Think: “What’s out there?” and “What’s reachable?”
Collect
Pulls public OSINT and historical URLs, then highlights interesting parameters that may matter for testing.
Think: “What evidence exists publicly?”
Flag
Runs lightweight checks to surface obvious misconfigurations or known exposures, then maps findings to OWASP categories.
Think: “What looks risky at a glance?”
Fast scan is designed for quick visibility. Deep scan goes further and takes longer. Only scan systems you own or have permission to test.
